About Me

About Me

Who am I?

Hi! I am Hieu Ngo, a.k.a. “enginoobz”, a Vietnamese and a final-year undergraduate Software Engineering student in Poland.

I'd consider myself a noob to remind me there're always new things to learn from anyone.

Background: Java & web development

Once upon a time, I took the first step on my programming journey with Java. Through some university classes combining self-education, I then explored various Java technologies, most notably Spring frameworks, for the development of enterprise & desktop software or back-end of web applications. I also slightly expanded my experiences learning a few web technologies for front-end development and built some of personal-fun-things.
If you ask me, Java and web development in general were all fun, untill I figured out my bigger passion…

Ambition: Video game development

While normal software is all about services to serve our life, computer graphics (CG) is the technology enabling us to create life itself, virtually. That interests me, Particularly, game development is a subset of CG, where we define our own rules for the world then allow players to get involved in. That interests me more.
I first got started in this field by playing around with a few CG and game tools. Feeling more and more engaged, I decided to pick Unity and Unreal Engine as my "long-term companions" on the journey.
Since then, I have been constantly horning my skills by registering for online courses, reading fundamental books, following bite-sized tutorials, solving coding problems, working on personal fun projects collaborative coding laboratories for my classes which involve with CG or game. While mainly focusing on gameplay programming, I enjoy every other aspect in the development process as well (as long as it's challenging). I especially fall in love with procedural generation, simulation, open world & 3D game genres.

What am I doing?

At the current time, I am exploring VR/AR and AI/ML fields – those technologies I believe that could bring huge enhancements for the experiences in games and CG projects in general.
I am also open for work in the game development to gain real-world and professional experience.
